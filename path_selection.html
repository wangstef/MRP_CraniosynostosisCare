Surgical Options

Click one of the surgical paths to learn more

Before 4 months old

Endoscopic Strip Craniectomy

Surgery before 4 months old

This surgery is minimally invasive where 2 small cuts are made on the child's head. It requires prolonged care over several checkups and helmet assessments. The skull will slowly change shape as it grows.

All ages

Cranial Vault Reshaping

Surgery typically before 12 months old

This is a more invasive surgery that remodels the skull. It requires intensive aftercare for a short period. It results in an immediate change to the skull shape needing less checkups and no helmet.
